Umar ibn Al-Khattāb

He was one of the most powerful and influential Muslim caliphs in history. He was a senior companion of the Islamic prophet Muhammad. He succeeded Abu Bakr (632–634) as the second caliph of the Rashidun Caliphate on 23 August 634. He was an expert Muslim jurist known for his pious and just nature.

Born: 584 AD, Mecca, Arabia

Died: 644 AD, Medina, Arabia
